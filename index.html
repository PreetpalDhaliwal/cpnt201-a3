Basketball

Basketball is a popular sport in this day and age. It was created by James Naismith. The game is played between two teams, each team has about 20-30 players. On the court, only 5 players from each team are allowed to play and switch with eachother during timeouts. The goal is to score more points than the opposing team by shooting the basketball into the hoop. Although the sport can be played indoors and outdoors, professional basketball is played indoors.
